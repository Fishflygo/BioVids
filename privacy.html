This Privacy Policy explains how fishfly ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service that creates and/or displays short biography videos about public figures.

1. Information We Collect

  • TikTok Login data (only if you connect TikTok): If you choose to connect your TikTok account, we receive only the information and permissions you authorize via TikTok Login (OAuth), such as basic account identifiers and the granted permission scopes. We do not access your TikTok data without your consent.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide the service features you request (including TikTok-connected features after authorization).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug issues.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • Comply with legal obligations where required.

3. Cookies

We may use essential cookies or similar technologies required for basic functionality (e.g., session management). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only in these cases:

  • Service providers: Hosting and security providers that help operate the service under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal & safety: If required by law, or to protect users, our service, and rights.
  • Business changes: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above. Security logs are typically kept for a limited period unless needed for investigating abuse or maintaining service integrity.

6. Your Choices

  • Disconnect TikTok: You can revoke our access at any time in your TikTok account settings (or within our service if available).
  • Requests: You may request access to or deletion of personal information we hold about you, where applicable.

Our service is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
